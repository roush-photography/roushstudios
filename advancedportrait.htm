This workshop is one that many of you have been waiting for me to put together.

This program will be different from other workshops in that you are invited and encouraged to bring your own lighting gear if you have it. Studio Strobes, Speedlight setups, umbrellas, softboxes, Backgrounds ect. We will have space and time for you to set up your gear and shoot/practice with it. You'll be able to ask questions about setting your gear up and using it in a studio situation. We will practice practice portraiture with your gear on your setup. I will have some models there for us to use. Plus, we can photograph each other as we learn.

Now, of course if you don't own studio lighting gear I will have mine set up for you to use to practice. I'll have two setups for use by students.

Things we will study:

portrait lightng Setups
Seniors
Couples
Groups
Engagement

Lighting Styles
High and Low Key Lighting
Umbrella Lighting
Softbox Lighting
Other lighting control gear - Snoots, Grids, hair-lights
Using your speedlight

Outdoor use of studio lighting in portraiture
Using Reflectors

These are just the main subjects of study.
we will dig in to them and study all the details.

If you have your own lighting gear to set up please arrive at 8am
This will allow you set up your equipment before the workshop starts at 9am

Everyone will also have the oppportunity to study all of the different types of lighting gear that is present. We should have 10-12 lighting setups to observe.

Around 4pm we've invited some of the church members to come to our workshop and be our portrait subjects.
So, with any luck you'll have some images to use as portfolio samples.
