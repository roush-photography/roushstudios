This advanced PhotoShop workshop is designed specifically for photographers.
Photoshop is a very complicated program .
It can be confusing. hard to learn. and difficult to understand.
Our day will be spent concentrating on the features of this massive program, covering specific techniques that a photographer needs to be familiar with to be competitive in this market.


We will review many tools and techniques photographers use every day. PhotoShop is the industry standard when it comes to digital image manipulation and retouching,
we will cover Logo Creation, Photography Promotional Mailers, and Image restoration.

Some of the techniques & topics we'll cover
in this workshop include:

Clipping Paths / Advanced Clipping
Paths Creating
your Business Logo
Business Cards
Creating Business Promotional Materials
Advanced color Replacement / Correction

Advanced Layering Replacing Skies, Raw Files
 Soft Focus Filters


For this workshop please bring your laptop and have photoshop installed.
If you don't have Photoshop please let me know and I can provide you with a copy
of the program to install on your PC.
Also make sure you have a "two-button" mouse with your laptop to use at the workshop.
Photoshop cannot be used effectively with the touch-pad on a laptop.
