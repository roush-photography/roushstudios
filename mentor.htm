a protégée (female), an apprentice or, in recent years, a mentee."

 
     
     
           
           
   

 

12 / 6 / 3 month Mentoring Program

This Mentoring Program is a new program set up for Photographers that feel they need and want some help but are not sure where to start. It is also perfect for the photographer who feels he/she is beyond being a beginner and doesn't want an entry level photography course.

Some photographers are somewhere in the middle and often don't fit the mold of an online course or a predefined photo program. With this program we can communicate "live" via phone / Skype / or one-on-one at minimum of two and a maximum of five times per month. We can talk about the problems and difficulties you might have with equipment or techniques instead of skipping over them like you may have already done.

If you want me to travel to your location (within reason) I can do so for minimal travel expense reimbursement. Every town has a coffee shop we can use as our meeting place. Often times during these meetings we can actually shoot photos that are part of our lessons. Wouldn't that be a great way to learn?

Also added to this program is a "free" consulation where you and I can talk and evaluate what it is you need to study, if anything. I have had a lot of folks want to sit down and talk with me about buying a new camera, some new lenses, some lighting gear, and/or any other photographic gear. This is a topic we can discuss during this meeting.

Here's how this program works -

1. When you sign up you're sent a simple questionnaire to fill out. This helps me know where you stand on your photographic education, what photo gear you have at your disposal, and your computer skills (Photoshop included).

2. Our first meeting / person to person discussion is about your gear (this can be in person or over the phone / skype). I need to know what you know about this equipment, so this is somewhat a fact-finding mission on my part.

3. I develop a personalized and definded program for you to follow based on the above knowlegde. It will be simple; you get lessons and assignments. You will do the assignments and send them to me to critique. If you do them right we move on. If you don't do them right we do them again. Before we start this program we review my suggestions about the educational program I've developed, so you know what you're in for over the next few months. We go slow, as to make sure you have the time to properly do the lessons and assignments. I can deliver lessons to you in what ever format you perfer. I can email them to you and you can read them, we can do them over the phone / Skype / or in person, or both.

4. At the end of the 12 month period we re-evaluate your photographic knowledge and decide together if we need to continue on or if you are at a place where you can continue on without me. I also have a "limited" mentor program if you want to keep the communication channels open for future months / years.
