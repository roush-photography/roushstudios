This workshop is one that many of you have been waiting for me to put together.

Wedding photography, although a tough event to cover, is one of the most profitable / money making nitches left in the photography business. Prices range from the low hundreds of dollars to thousands of dollars for coverage. This workshop will help you figure out how to do them, how to price them, and how to sell them.

This is an "in-depth" study of the whole wedding event. We'll dicuss the "before" pictures, the engagement portrait, the processional, ceremony & vows, recessional, and the reception. We'll talk about tricks to help you produce great images and also tricks to keep things under control before, during, and after.

Weddings are tough, but also very much a money maker for photographers if you handle it properly.

The first half of this workshop will be lecture. We'll discuss all the details of the wedding process from start to finish. The afternoon portion of this workshop will be a re-inactment of an actual wedding in a church. We'll have a bride, groom, and some attendants to work with as we all photograph this mock wedding event. You get to feel what's it's like to have a bride walking down the isle toward you. And, you get a chance to photograph the service part, as we let you find photographs around the church to capture the ceremony.

We will then set up for the formal portraits in the front of the church after the ceremony is over. We'll will go through the list of "necessary portraits" that you should take and then also some easy to do custom looking ideas that sell prints to the client. After this we'll discuss the reception and what the important images are that you need to make sure you get.

All in all, after this workshop is over you should have a pretty good idea of what you need to do, what gear you need to produce the images, and a pretty good game plan on making it all look beautiful.
