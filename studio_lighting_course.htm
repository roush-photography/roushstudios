Studio Lighting Course

Families, Seniors, Groups, Babies
Concentration on Setups and Lighting
In your Studio / Shooting Space
_________________________________________________

This Course, unlike my other programs is a course where I visit you in person and help you with your studio setups, lighting, posing, compositions, backgrounds, and any other things you feel you need help with.

I will meet with you initially to go over some basic materials with lighting, studio setups, and posing. Our next 3 meetings will be at your location / studio. I will stay for an hour each time and help you with set ups, lighting styles, and posing. You are responsible to provide models for these sessions if you want them.

We will cover Studio Strobes, Continuous Lighting, Speedlight setups, umbrellas, softboxes, Backgrounds ect. We will set up your gear and shoot/practice with it. You'll be able to ask questions about setting up your photo gear and using it in your studio situation.
